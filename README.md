# Varinda Store Annual Report 2022 (Excel Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Excel dashboard** built to analyze Varinda Store’s annual performance for 2022.  
The dashboard highlights **sales trends, customer demographics, order status, and regional performance** using Excel’s advanced features.

The main objective was to transform raw retail data into **clear, actionable business insights**.

---

## 🔹 Key KPIs
- Total Sales Revenue  
- Customer Demographics (Male vs Female)  
- Order Status (Delivered, Returned, Cancelled)  
- Sales by State & City  
- Sales by Channel (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)  
- Age Group Contribution  

---

## 🔹 Dashboard Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend (Jan–Dec)  
- State-wise Sales Distribution (Map Visualization)  
- Sales by Gender (Pie Chart)  
- Order Status Breakdown  
- Top Performing States & Cities  
- Sales Channel Contribution  
- Age Group Analysis

---

## 📈 Insights
- **March 2022** recorded the highest sales (~₹1.9M).  
- **Women customers** contributed ~64% of total sales.  
- **92% orders delivered successfully**, showing strong fulfillment.  
- **Bengaluru (₹1.77M)** and **Hyderabad (₹1.37M)** were top-performing states.  
- **Amazon (35%)** and **Myntra (23%)** dominated sales channels.  
- Adults (~35%) formed the largest buyer segment.
